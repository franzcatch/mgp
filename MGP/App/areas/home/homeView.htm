MGP MISSION

 

MGP is committed to financially help Latin-American communities, with low availability of economic resources, by creating micro-enterprises and businesses, promoting education, culture, and values through the efforts of all individuals, public and private institutions, aiding the development of socio-economic conditions.

MGP VISION

 

The Millennium Global Prosperity (MGP) seeks to achieve wealth creation, social and economic development to assist low income Latinos in becoming a part of the global market. It is through these practices that MGP will revive core humanitarian values in Latin American communities, and accomplish ideal community development.

MGP MAJOR OBJECTIVES

  • Increase awareness and promotion of wealth creation in Latin America and the United States.
  • Implement research studies and develop initiatives to identify best business and organizational practices.
  • Promote global campaigns to increase prosperity and eradicate poverty.
  • Initiate self-help programs through partnership building and collaboration.
  • Educate and inform population regarding causes of poverty and hunger.
  • Facilitate the transfer of skills, goods, and services to Latin America to support the fight against poverty and hunger.
  • Promote a US - Latin American partnership of information sharing, networking and collaborative initiatives among organizations engaged in the fight against poverty and hunger.
